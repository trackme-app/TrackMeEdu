# Attendance Service

## Overview
The Attendance Service logs and monitors the presence of students and staff within the Education Management System. It provides data for reporting and trigger alerts for absentees.

## Key Features
- **Daily Attendance**: Record attendance for classes and events.
- **Biometric Integration**: Support for biometric or card-based entry (optional).
- **Absence Reporting**: Notify parents or supervisors of absence.
- **Stats & Reports**: Calculate attendance percentages and identify trends.
