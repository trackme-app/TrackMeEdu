# Operations & Communication Domain

## Overview
This domain facilitates the day-to-day operations and communication flows within the institution, ensuring smooth logistical and financial management.

## Services
- **Attendance**: Tracks student and staff attendance.
- **Finance & Fees**: Manages tuition fees, payments, and financial reporting.
- **Library**: Manages library resources, book loans, and digital assets.
- **Notifications**: Centralized service for sending alerts, emails, and SMS to users.
