# Academic & Learning Domain

## Overview
This domain encompasses services focused on the core educational mission of the system. It handles curriculum planning, course delivery, assessments, and scheduling.

## Services
- **Assessment & Grading**: Manages student assessments, exams, and grading records.
- **Course & Curriculum**: Handles course creation, curriculum mapping, and syllabus management.
- **Learning Management System (LMS)**: Facilitates online learning, assignment submissions, and resource sharing.
- **Scheduling & Timetable**: Manages class schedules, room bookings, and exam timetables.
